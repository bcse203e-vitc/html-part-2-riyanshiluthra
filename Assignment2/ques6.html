Chicken Curry

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add the curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Add the chicken pieces, season with salt, and cook until the chicken is browned.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories per serving: 550 kcal
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbs: 45g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 6g

Fun Fact

Chicken curry is a popular dish in many countries and varies greatly in flavor and preparation methods across different regions.
